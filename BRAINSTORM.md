# HabitFC — Feature Brainstorm

## Core Loop (current)
**habits → coins → packs → cards**

Missing piece: cards need a *purpose* beyond collecting. Team Builder closes that loop.

---

## 🏟️ Team Builder (Priority 1)
- Pick your XI from owned cards, set a formation (4-3-3, 4-4-2, etc.)
- Visual pitch — players shown as card thumbnails on a football field layout
- Team Overall — calculated from player stats (PAC, SHO, PAS, DRI averaged + weighted by position)
- Chemistry — same club or nationality gives a stats boost, encourages collecting strategically
- New `/team` route

## ⚽ Matches / Auto-battles (Priority 2)
- Weekly fixture — team auto-plays against a generated opponent
- Result determined by Team Overall + current Form
- Win = bonus coins, maybe a guaranteed card drop
- Simple result screen: "Динамо Київ 3–1 Real Madrid" with scorers

## 🏆 Achievements / Milestones (Priority 3)
- "Complete 7-day streak" → unlock a special gold card
- "Own 10 legendaries" → trophy
- "Build a full squad from one club" → chemistry bonus pack
- Achievements give collection direction and long-term goals

## 🔨 Card Upgrades (Priority 4)
- Duplicates become shards of that rarity
- Spend shards to upgrade a card's stats (+5 PAC, etc.)
- Gives duplicates value instead of just a coin refund

## 🔥 Habit → Gameplay Integration (Priority 5)
- Habit streaks → Team Form (1–10), rises with completions, drops on misses
- Habit categories (fitness/mind/creativity) → boost specific player roles
- Completing fitness habits boosts forwards; mind habits boost midfielders

## 📅 Challenges / Events (Priority 6)
- Weekly rotating challenge: "Complete 5 fitness habits → earn this limited card"
- Limited-time packs with themed players
- Keeps daily engagement high

---

## Suggested Roadmap

| Phase | Feature | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Team Builder | Closes the loop, most impactful |
| 2 | Matches | Gives the team a purpose |
| 3 | Achievements | Long-term goals, easy to build |
| 4 | Card Upgrades | Duplicates feel less bad |
| 5 | Habit → Form link | Deep integration, most satisfying |
| 6 | Challenges/Events | Retention & engagement |
