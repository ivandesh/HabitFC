Squad Battle — Core Concept

How should the battle flow work? Pick the approach that excites you most.

A

⚡ Quick Match — Instant Result

Tap "Battle" → animated scoreboard reveals final score with key stats (possession, shots, etc.). Fast and punchy. Timeline shows 3-5 key moments (goals, red cards) as a summary after the result.

Best for: casual play, quick dopamine hits, mobile-first
B

📺 Live Simulation — Minute-by-Minute

Watch the match unfold in real-time (accelerated). A timeline ticks from 0' to 90', with events appearing as they happen — goals, cards, substitutions, near-misses. Takes ~30-60 seconds to play out.

Best for: immersion, tension, "watching" the game
C

🎬 Hybrid — Result + Replay

Calculate result instantly, then show a cinematic replay. Score revealed with dramatic animation, followed by a scrollable match timeline with all events. User controls the pace — can skip or savor.

Best for: flexibility, replayability, best of both worlds

Context for all approaches:

  • Your squad (11 players + formation + coach) vs friend's squad
  • Winner calculation uses player stats, chemistry, coach perks, formation matchups, + RNG
  • Same squads can produce different results each time
